Chicken Pesto

Description

Chicken pesto is a perfect spring/summer meal that is perfect for a quick dinner or a delicious meal that can be made a head of time.

    Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Bell Peppers (2)
  • Yellow Squash
  • Zucchini
  • Frozen Green Beans
  • Boneless Chicken Breast (1 pound)
  • Red Onion
  • Pesto

Let's get cooking!

  1. Take all your fresh vegetables and cut into like sized pieces
  2. Now take your chicken, and do the same! Before you do this, start heating up your skillet on a medium-high heat!
  3. Once the skillet is hot, put olive oil in and let heat up. Throw chicken in and cook for 4-5 minutes. Don't cook through yet, chicken will continue cooking as you cook your veg.
  4. Next add the frozen green beans, after 2-3 minutes then add red onion.
  5. Then add your bell pepper, squash, and zucchini.
  6. After all of this has cooked down, turn heat off and add your desired amount of pesto and mix in.
  7. That is it, you've made a simple yet filling dish. You can add a side of pasta or whatever tickles your fancy, go wild
